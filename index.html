Research


Data Obfuscation

2015 - Present

The problem of data obfuscation deals with the tools and techniques developed to ensure privacy and utility guarantee to a data release mechanism. At first the data getting released publicly must be given some privacy guarantee and then making statistical inferences from the modified data-set is the challenge of data obfuscation.

Presently, I am involved in two research projects, one is a NTRO funded project assigned to RC Bose Centre for Cryptology and Security (RCBCCS), ISI and the other is funded by National Institute of Information and Communications Technology (NICT).
